Rewards

From time to time, the project may allocate ETH to the community rewards contract.

Each reward round distributes ETH equally across all 333 BullRun Key NFTs. Holders can claim available rewards directly from the smart contract.

  • rewards are distributed in ETH
  • each NFT receives an equal share
  • claim period lasts 365 days
  • unclaimed rewards may later be recycled into future community reward rounds

FAQ

How do rewards work?
The creator periodically deposits ETH into the reward contract. Each BullRun Key NFT receives an equal share that can be claimed by the holder.
How often are reward rounds funded?
Reward rounds may be funded approximately every 1–3 months depending on market conditions and project activity.
How long can rewards be claimed?
Each reward round has a claim window of 365 days.
What happens to unclaimed rewards?
After the claim period ends, unclaimed ETH may be reclaimed by the project and redistributed in future reward rounds.
Where can I buy BullRun Key?
BullRun Keys are available on the secondary market on OpenSea.
