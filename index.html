FAQ

How do rewards work?
The creator periodically deposits ETH into the reward contract. BullRun Key holders can claim available rewards according to the active reward logic.
WHO CAN CLAIM REWARDS?
Rewards can only be claimed by the wallet that currently holds the BullRun Key NFT at the moment of claiming.
How often are reward rounds funded?
Reward rounds may be funded depending on treasury growth, market conditions, and project activity.
How long can rewards be claimed?
Each reward round has a claim window of 365 days.
WHAT HAPPENS TO UNCLAIMED REWARDS?
Unclaimed rewards remain claimable for 365 days. After the claim period ends, unclaimed ETH may be reclaimed by the project owner and used for future ecosystem activities or future reward rounds.
What happens at 1 ETH treasury?
The first 1 ETH treasury milestone is planned to unlock Reward Round #1 and the full BullRun Key reveal.
Where can I buy BullRun Key?
BullRun Keys are available on the secondary market on OpenSea.
